Who We Are

Robinson Painting Company was formed on the foundation of a single value: family. We believe that family is the most important thing we are given and, as a family owned and operated business, we are driven by our desire to create better, cleaner, and more comforting spaces for you and your family to grow.

I'm Tim Robinson and I like to make it a point to treat every room and every home we paint with the same care and attention I would expect for my own.

What We Do

We paint interior and exterior, residential and commercial. Our team is skilled in every aspect of the painting process so that you can be sure the projects we undertake will be done well.

It is our priority to guarantee safety and quality in every project we undertake. We strive to make what can be, and often is, a frustrating process much much easier. Painting can be a pain, but we make it our job to ensure that the entirety of it all, start to finish, is 100% hassle-free.

Worried about that room that needs a little touch more than a fresh coat of paint? Our team is experienced in a wide range of services beyond painting, from drywall installation to wallpaper removal, so that you don't have to go through the trouble to find someone else to take care of those jobs.
