There's something about the way

letters look when they are beside one another,

the way words take shape by their being beside one another,

and how, by words, we are able to do, create, and say so much with little more than the stroke of a pen.

I graduated Anderson University with a degree in English Creative Writing and, ever since, all I've ever wanted has been to

adapt the flexibility of the words we use to build unique and memorable things.

That's why I do what I do.

Web Development is, in its truest sense, the beauty of what letter after letter,

word after word, laid end to end,

is capable of creating.

Projects

Robinson Painting Company

Robinson Painting Co. is a business in upstate South Carolina that specializes in home and commercial, interior and exterior painting, as well as a handful of other repair and restoration services. The business, at the moment, only has a FaceBook page to advertise their work, but I decided to take it upon myself to mockup a website for them. That being said, the structure of the website and the words on it do not necessarily represent the style or views of the company, but rather are choices I made and words I chose that I best believed would capture the tone of what they are doing. The photos are the property of Rhythm and Ash Photography. You can see the mockup I did right here and their official FaceBook page here.
